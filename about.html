About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of men who follow Jesus and want to share the Good News with others.

What we do

We collaborate and support communities and organization to glorify the Lord and build the Kingdom

Vision

To share the Good News of Jesus Christ and to Disciple men to be fit for the mission.

Mission

To Spiritually prepare men to live a healthy life and walk in the Authority of Jesus by helping to provide the necassary biblical tools.

Purpose

To work alongside men in thier personal journey of growth in Jesus Christ and support others in walking in obedience to what God is calling them to glorify and build the Kingdom
